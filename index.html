Why 9 Waves?

The number 9, along with the number 3, is everywhere in Celtic mythology. Nine waves was the distance the Milesian Gaels, invading from what's now Spain, were ordered to keep from the Irish shore by the Tuath Dé Danann, the magical race who then inhabited Ireland. When the shivering warriors made it to shore, it was "over nine waves".

When doing your cold immersion (in ice bath or in wild water) try settling in for nine deep, slow breaths. If it doesn't wake something mythical in you, nothing will.
